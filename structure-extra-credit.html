myPlate

MyPlate is a reminder to find your healthy eating style and build it throughout your lifetime. Everything you eat and drink matters. The rigt mix can help you be healthier now and in the future. This means:

  • Focus on variety, amount and nutritional
  • Choose foods and beveraes with less saturated fat, sodium and added sugars
  • Start with small changes to build healthier eating styles
  • Support healthy eating for everyone
